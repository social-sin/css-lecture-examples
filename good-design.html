Typography & Readability

Good typography improves readability through spacing, contrast, consistent sizing, and comfortable line-height. Clear typography reduces reading fatigue and improves comprehension.

BAD TYPOGRAPHY can become difficult to read when spacing, contrast, sizing, or alignment are inconsistent. 

p {
  line-height: 1.7;
  font-size: 1rem;
}

Readability is more important than decoration.
