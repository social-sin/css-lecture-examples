Overflow

The overflow property controls what happens when content exceeds the size of its container.

overflow: visible;

This content extends outside the container because overflow is visible.

overflow: hidden;

This content is clipped because overflow is hidden.

overflow: scroll;

Scrollbars always appear even when not needed.

overflow: auto;

Scrollbars only appear when content exceeds the container size.

z-index (Stacking Order)

Higher z-index values appear in front of lower values.

z-index: 1
